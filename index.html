Mobile App Development
Pass the Auxx..
Pass the Auxx is a mobile applicaton designed to eliminate the issue of a party silence when people have to pass the aux cord. The app allows perty-goers to join a lobby, queue up music, vote to skip, and upvote songs... all without having to break the vibe.
Web Spider
Visualizing Navigable Links (VNL)
Visualizing Navigable Links or VNL is a web page spider designed to comb through the DOM of html pages and expand to each of the navigable links on that page, recording the journey. The end goal of VNL was to display the resulting navigation tree and allow the user to determine the degrees of seperation between two pages in the visualization.
